Types of Psychiatric Disorders

Schizophrenia

Schizophrenia is a chronic and severe mental disorder that affects a person's ability to think, feel, and behave clearly.

Dissociative identity disorder

Dissociative identity disorder (DID), formerly known as multiple personality disorder, is a mental disorder characterized by the presence of two or more distinct personality states, often referred to as "alters."

Attention-deficit/hyperactivity disorder

Attention-deficit/hyperactivity disorder (ADHD) is a neurodevelopmental condition impacting attention span, impulsivity, and hyperactivity.
